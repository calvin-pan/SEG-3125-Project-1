Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, our hair salon has a cancellation policy in place. We understand that circumstances may arise where you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, and we strive to accommodate our clients' needs while also ensuring the smooth operation of our salon. Our cancellation policy requires that you provide us with at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. This allows us to offer the time slot to another client who may be waiting for an appointment. In the event that you fail to provide the required notice or miss your appointment without prior notification, we may apply a cancellation fee. The specific fee amount may depend on the length and type of the scheduled service. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation in adhering to our cancellation policy. If you have any further questions or need to cancel or reschedule an appointment, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're here to assist you and provide exceptional service.

If you are running late for your appointment at our hair salon, we kindly request that you notify us as soon as possible. While we understand that unforeseen circumstances can sometimes cause delays, punctuality is important to ensure that we can provide you with the best service and maintain a smooth schedule for all our clients. Depending on the circumstances and the availability of our stylists, we will do our best to accommodate your appointment. However, please note that if you arrive significantly late, it may impact the length of your appointment or the services that can be performed within the remaining time. In some cases, we may need to reschedule your appointment to ensure that we can serve you and other clients efficiently. We value the time of all our clients, and making adjustments may be necessary to maintain a timely schedule and provide quality service to everyone. We encourage you to arrive a few minutes before your scheduled appointment time to allow for check-in and any necessary preparations. If you anticipate being late or need to reschedule, please contact us promptly so that we can assist you accordingly. Our goal is to provide you with an exceptional salon experience, and we appreciate your understanding and cooperation regarding punctuality and appointment scheduling.
